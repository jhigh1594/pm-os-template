# Internal Enablement for Product Launches

Your internal teams need to be ready before customers are.

---

## Enablement Philosophy

**The Rule:** Every customer-facing team should learn about the launch *before* customers do.

## Team-by-Team Enablement

### 1. Customer Success Briefing

**Goal:** CS can proactively drive adoption and handle customer questions.

```markdown
# CS Briefing Template: [Feature Name]

## Quick Reference (1-pager)

### What Is It?
[1-2 sentence description in customer-facing language]

### Who It's For
- **Best fit:** [Ideal customer profile]
- **Trigger for outreach:** [What signals this customer needs it]

### Key Benefits
1. [Benefit 1 with customer language]
2. [Benefit 2 with customer language]
3. [Benefit 3 with customer language]

### How to Position
"In the latest release, we added [feature]. This helps teams [benefit]. I thought of you because [personalization]."

---

## Detailed Briefing (for team meeting)

### Business Context
- **Why we built this:** [Customer demand, competitive gap, strategic priority]
- **Target segment:** [Who we're going after]
- **Revenue impact:** [Upsell opportunity, expansion revenue, competitive wins]

### Customer Impact
- **Pain it solves:** [Specific problem]
- **Before/after:** [How their workflow changes]
- **Time to value:** [How quickly they see benefit]

### Adoption Strategy
- **Priority accounts:** [Top 10-20 accounts to contact]
- **Outreach timing:** [When to contact]
- **Email template:** [See below]

### Common Questions

A: [Pricing/packaging answer]

Q: "How do I get started?"
A: [Onboarding steps]

A: [Demo booking process or self-serve]
```

## Enablement Checklist by Timeline

### T-3 Weeks Before Launch
- [ ] Enablement materials drafted
- [ ] CS and Support leads reviewed materials
- [ ] Sales enablement reviewed by top reps

### T-2 Weeks Before Launch
- [ ] Sales training scheduled
- [ ] CS briefing scheduled
- [ ] Support playbook finalized
- [ ] FAQ document ready

### T-1 Week Before Launch
- [ ] Sales training complete
- [ ] CS briefing complete
- [ ] Support briefed
- [ ] Engineering on-call assigned
- [ ] Internal Slack channel created

### Launch Day
- [ ] All-hands announcement (if Tier 1)
- [ ] Slack channel active for questions
- [ ] Support monitoring for issues
- [ ] CS outreach to priority accounts

### T+1 Week After Launch
- [ ] Collect internal feedback
- [ ] Update FAQ based on questions
- [ ] Share early metrics with teams
- [ ] Address any enablement gaps
