## When to Use External Research Tools

For live competitive pricing data or current market benchmarks, use web search tools (Perplexity, Exa, or web search) to gather:
- Current competitor pricing pages
- Recent pricing change announcements
- Industry benchmark reports
- Analyst coverage of pricing trends

Always label externally sourced data with the retrieval date and source URL.
